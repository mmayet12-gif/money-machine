# Honest Expectations

## About the Uploader

The uploader uploads as Private. You publish manually after review. This is
not laziness or paranoia — every uploaded video is a chance to catch:

- A TTS pronunciation error that makes you sound stupid
- A factual mistake that gets you ratio'd in the comments
- A copyright claim on background music
- A thumbnail you forgot to add
- A title that's wrong because the script generator drifted from the topic

If you skip the review step you will eventually publish something embarrassing
or wrong. The 30 seconds it takes to glance at the video before flipping to
Public is the cheapest insurance available.
