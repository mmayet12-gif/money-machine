**Product Concept Pack**

**Product Name:** 5 Money Mistakes I Made in My 20s - Learn from My Experience to Avoid Financial Regret

**Product Concept Statement:**
"5 Money Mistakes I Made in My 20s is a digital product that provides working adults aged 25-45 with practical guidance on avoiding costly financial mistakes made during their young adult years. By sharing his personal experience and expertise, the creator helps users take control of their finances, break free from debt, and build a stable financial foundation for long-term success."

**Audience Outcome:**

* Identify common money mistakes made in the 20s
* Understand the cost impact of these mistakes on long-term financial goals
* Learn corrective actions to rectify past financial decisions
* Develop a plan to avoid similar mistakes and achieve financial stability

**Module Outline:**

1. **Mistake 1:** Not Creating an Emergency Fund
	* Cost Impact: Years of living paycheck-to-paycheck, delayed financial milestones
	* Corrective Action: Setting up a 3-6 month emergency fund
	* Next Steps: Prioritizing savings and automating transfers
2. **Mistake 2:** Accumulating High-Interest Debt
	* Cost Impact: Thousands of dollars in interest payments, damaged credit score
	* Corrective Action: Consolidating debt, negotiating with creditors
	* Next Steps: Creating a debt repayment plan and sticking to it
3. **Mistake 3:** Not Investing for Retirement
	* Cost Impact: Reduced savings potential, delayed retirement goals
	* Corrective Action: Starting a retirement account (e.g., IRA, 401(k))
	* Next Steps: Automating contributions and exploring employer matching
4. **Mistake 4:** Not Prioritizing Financial Education
	* Cost Impact: Missed opportunities for financial growth, poor decision-making
	* Corrective Action: Seeking financial advice, reading personal finance books
	* Next Steps: Continuing education and community engagement
5. **Mistake 5:** Not Reviewing and Adjusting Finances Regularly
	* Cost Impact: Inefficient spending, missed savings opportunities
	* Corrective Action: Creating a budget, tracking expenses, and adjusting regularly
	* Next Steps: Implementing regular financial reviews and adjustments

**Deliverables List:**

1. 5-Module Video Course (approx. 60 minutes total)
2. Downloadable Worksheets for each Module (e.g., emergency fund tracker, debt repayment plan)
3. Bonus Webinar: "Beyond the Mistakes" - Strategies for long-term financial success
4. Exclusive Community Access for Support and Accountability

**Pricing Hypothesis:** $297 - $497 (mid-tier pricing to balance value proposition with price sensitivity)

**Launch Checklist:**

1. **Pre-Launch:**
	* Content creation and review (module videos, worksheets, webinar)
	* Email marketing campaign setup
	* Social media promotion plan
2. **Launch Day:**
	* Email sequence deployment (welcome, module 1 introduction, etc.)
	* Social media promotions and engagement
	* Exclusive community access for early registrants
3. **Post-Launch:**
	* Review and improve content based on user feedback
	* Develop a content calendar for future modules and bonus materials
	* Continue social media promotion and engagement

This product concept pack provides a clear roadmap for creating a valuable digital product that addresses the specific financial challenges faced by working adults in their 20s. By leveraging personal experience, practical guidance, and community support, this product is poised to help users avoid costly mistakes and achieve long-term financial stability.
