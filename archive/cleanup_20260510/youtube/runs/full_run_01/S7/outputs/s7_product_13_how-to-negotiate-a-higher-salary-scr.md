**Product Concept Pack**

**1. Product Concept Statement**

"Empower working adults to secure a higher salary with confidence through a step-by-step negotiation guide and script library, ensuring they receive the financial recognition they deserve."

**2. Audience Outcome**

By purchasing "How to Negotiate a Higher Salary (Scripts Included)", our target audience of working adults aged 25-45 will be able to:

* Develop effective communication skills for salary negotiations
* Confidently articulate their value and worth to employers
* Achieve an average salary increase of $5,000-$10,000 per year

**3. Module Outline**

The digital product will consist of four modules, each focusing on a critical aspect of successful salary negotiation:

1. **Prep**
	* Understanding your worth: Identifying strengths and achievements
	* Researching market value: Salaries for similar roles in your industry
	* Setting goals and expectations: Creating a clear vision for the negotiation
2. **Script Examples**
	* Introduction scripts: Confidently introducing yourself and your goals
	* Value scripts: Clearly articulating your contributions to the company
	* Request scripts: Crafting a convincing request for a higher salary
3. **Pushback Handling**
	* Common objections: Anticipating and addressing concerns from employers
	* Reframing pushback: Turning negatives into positives
	* Negotiation strategies: Using verbal cues and tone to influence outcomes
4. **Follow-up**
	* Creating a negotiation plan: Ensuring progress toward your goals
	* Managing emotions: Staying calm and composed during negotiations
	* Review and adjust: Refining your approach for future salary talks

**4. Deliverables List**

The product will include:

* A comprehensive 30-page guide (PDF) outlining the negotiation process and script examples
* 10+ video lessons (mp4) covering key topics, including intro scripts and value statements
* Downloadable templates for tracking progress and creating a negotiation plan
* Access to a private Facebook group or community forum for Q&A and support

**5. Pricing Hypothesis**

Based on industry benchmarks and the perceived value of the product, we will initially price "How to Negotiate a Higher Salary (Scripts Included)" at $297-$497, with potential discounts offered for bundle purchases or loyalty rewards.

**6. Launch Checklist**

To ensure a successful launch:

1. **Content creation**: Develop and finalize all content modules.
2. **Marketing materials**: Design eye-catching visuals, social media assets, and email marketing campaigns.
3. **Email list building**: Grow a targeted audience through lead magnets, webinars, or guest blogging.
4. **Pre-launch promotion**: Share behind-the-scenes insights, sneak peeks, or exclusive offers to generate buzz.
5. **Launch sequence**: Coordinate automated email sequences for sales, delivery, and follow-up support.
6. **Post-launch evaluation**: Monitor analytics, gather feedback, and refine the product and marketing strategy as needed.

This product concept pack provides a solid foundation for creating a valuable digital product that empowers working adults to negotiate higher salaries with confidence.
