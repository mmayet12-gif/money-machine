**Product Concept Pack**

**1. Product Concept Statement**

Title: "Brokerage Academy"

Tagline: "Master the art of investing with confidence"

Concept Statement: The Brokerage Academy is an online course designed to educate working adults on how to set up and manage their brokerage accounts effectively, minimizing costly mistakes and maximizing returns.

**2. Audience Outcome**

By completing the Brokerage Academy, our audience will be able to:

* Understand the basics of brokerage accounts and how they work
* Make informed decisions when choosing a broker
* Invest wisely in their first investments
* Manage taxes associated with investing

Audience Goals: Reduce financial stress, increase confidence in investing, and achieve long-term financial stability.

**3. Module Outline**

Module 1: Brokerage Account Basics (30 minutes)

* What is a brokerage account?
* Types of accounts (e.g., cash, margin, IRA)
* Fees associated with different types of accounts

Module 2: Choosing the Right Broker (45 minutes)

* Factors to consider when selecting a broker
* Top-rated brokers for beginners
* Tips for evaluating broker services and fees

Module 3: Investing in Your First Portfolio (60 minutes)

* Understanding asset allocation
* Selecting your first investments (stocks, ETFs, etc.)
* Diversification strategies for beginners

Module 4: Tax Essentials for Investors (45 minutes)

* Introduction to tax basics for investing
* Capital gains and losses explained
* Strategies for minimizing taxes on investment income

**4. Deliverables List**

* 4-module online course (video-based with interactive elements)
* Downloadable cheat sheet: "Brokerage Account Basics"
* Access to a private community forum for support and Q&A

**5. Pricing Hypothesis**

Based on research, we hypothesize that the optimal price point for the Brokerage Academy will be $297. This is higher than other beginner-focused courses due to the comprehensive nature of our content and the value proposition offered.

**6. Launch Checklist**

* Finalize course content and formatting (4-6 weeks)
* Design and develop online learning platform (2-3 weeks)
* Set up affiliate partnerships with top-rated brokers
* Launch marketing campaigns on social media, email, and relevant forums (2-4 weeks)
* Monitor and optimize course performance after launch

**Additional Considerations**

* Affiliate income will be generated through partner brokerages' referral fees.
* Ongoing maintenance and updates to the course content will ensure its relevance and accuracy.

This product concept pack provides a solid foundation for creating a high-quality online course that addresses a specific need in the personal finance space. By focusing on education and practical advice, we can build trust with our audience and establish ourselves as thought leaders in the brokerage account beginners niche.
