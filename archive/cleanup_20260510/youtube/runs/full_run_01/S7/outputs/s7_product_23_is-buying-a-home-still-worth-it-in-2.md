**Product Concept Pack: "Home Buying Calculator 2026"**

**Product Concept Statement:**
Home Buying Calculator 2026 is an online tool that helps working adults (25-45) make informed decisions about whether to buy or rent a home in 2026, taking into account updated cost models and hidden expenses.

**Audience Outcome:**
Users will have a clear understanding of the financial implications of buying vs. renting a home in 2026, empowering them to make an informed decision that aligns with their personal goals and priorities.

**Module Outline:**

1. **Cost Model**: Users input current income, location, and credit score to estimate monthly mortgage payments, property taxes, insurance, and maintenance costs.
2. **Hidden Costs**: Calculates additional expenses like homeowner association fees, utility bills, and renovation costs.
3. **Break-Even Point**: Provides a personalized analysis of when the user will break even on their investment (e.g., "You'll pay off your mortgage in 10 years").
4. **Decision Rubric**: Offers a simple, interactive framework to weigh pros and cons, considering factors like financial stability, long-term goals, and personal preferences.

**Deliverables:**

1. Interactive online calculator tool
2. In-depth user guide with explanations and examples
3. Email course (5-day) with daily lessons on budgeting for homeownership
4. Access to a private Facebook group or community forum for ongoing support

**Pricing Hypothesis:**
$297 (limited-time launch offer), increasing to $497 after 30 days, and $997 for premium features and personalized coaching.

**Launch Checklist:**

1. Develop the calculator tool using Google Sheets or Airtable
2. Create high-quality visuals and graphics for the landing page and marketing materials
3. Set up email marketing automation (e.g., Mailchimp) for the course and community
4. Establish a presence on social media platforms (Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn)
5. Plan and schedule content promotion on relevant subreddits and online forums

**Launch Timeline:**

* Week 1-2: Develop calculator tool and create marketing materials
* Week 3-4: Set up email marketing automation and establish social media presence
* Week 5-6: Launch website, announce product launch, and start content promotion

This concept pack provides a realistic scope for a solo creator to develop and launch an online tool that addresses the changing math of buying vs. renting a home in 2026. By prioritizing speed to first sale, we can iterate on the product based on user feedback and adapt to market conditions while maximizing revenue potential through affiliate marketing and advertising.
