**Product Concept Pack: How to Get Out of Debt Fast on Low Income**

**Product Concept Statement:**
"How to Get Out of Debt Fast on Low Income" is an online course that helps working adults with low incomes break free from debt using a step-by-step process, empowering them to take control of their financial lives.

**Audience Outcome:**
Within 30 days, learners will have created a personalized plan to pay off debt and achieve financial stability, even on a limited income. By the end of the course, they'll be able to:

* Shift their mindset towards financial freedom
* Create a bare-bones budget that works for them
* Identify opportunities to generate extra cash
* Prioritize debt repayment using a proven sequence

**Module Outline:**

1. **Mindset Reset**
	* Understanding the psychology of debt
	* Building motivation and discipline
	* Setting realistic goals
2. **Bare-Bones Budgeting**
	* Tracking expenses and creating a budget
	* Prioritizing essential expenses
	* Cutting unnecessary costs
3. **Extra Cash Ideas**
	* Side hustles for low-income earners
	* Selling unwanted items and decluttering
	* Reducing expenses through negotiation and research
4. **Payoff Sequence**
	* Identifying debt priorities (debt avalanche vs. snowball)
	* Creating a debt repayment plan
	* Strategies for staying on track

**Deliverables:**

1. 5 video lessons, each 20-30 minutes long
2. Downloadable worksheets and templates:
	* Budget tracker
	* Debt repayment plan template
	* Extra cash ideas worksheet
3. Private online community for support and accountability
4. Email support for 30 days

**Pricing Hypothesis:**
Based on similar courses in the personal finance niche, we'll start with a price point of $297, targeting high-ticket sales to maximize revenue. We'll offer discounts for early bird registrations (e.g., $197) and bundle deals for premium content (e.g., one-on-one coaching).

**Launch Checklist:**

1. Content creation:
	* Record video lessons
	* Design and prepare worksheets and templates
2. Platform setup:
	* Choose a learning management system (LMS)
	* Set up email marketing automation
3. Marketing strategy:
	* Social media promotion
	* Influencer partnerships
	* Paid advertising (Facebook, Google Ads)
4. Pre-launch preparation:
	* Build an email list and offer early bird registration incentives
	* Prepare launch-day content (social media posts, blog articles)
5. Launch day:
	* Send out a launch email with exclusive offers and links to the course
	* Promote the course on social media and via email marketing

This product concept pack is designed to help you create a comprehensive online course that addresses the specific needs of working adults struggling with debt on low incomes. By focusing on clear, practical, and motivational content, you'll empower your audience to take control of their finances and achieve financial stability.
