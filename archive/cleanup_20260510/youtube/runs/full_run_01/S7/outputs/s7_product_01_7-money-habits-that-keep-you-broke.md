Here's a product concept pack for "7 Money Habits That Keep You Broke":

**Product Concept Statement**

"7 Money Habits That Keep You Broke" is an online course and community that helps working adults (25-45) break free from bad money habits and achieve financial stability. Through clear, practical, and motivational content, users will learn to identify and overcome their financial obstacles, creating a sustainable path to wealth.

**Audience Outcome**

By the end of the 7-day challenge, users will be able to:

* Identify and break free from at least one bad money habit
* Develop a basic understanding of personal finance management
* Create a personalized budget and start making progress towards their financial goals
