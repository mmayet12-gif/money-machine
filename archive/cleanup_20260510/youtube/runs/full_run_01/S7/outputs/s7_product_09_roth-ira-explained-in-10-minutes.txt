**Product Concept Pack for "Roth IRA Explained in 10 Minutes"**

**Product Concept Statement**
Create a digital product that explains the Roth Individual Retirement Account (IRA) in an easy-to-understand format, helping working adults save and grow their retirement funds efficiently.

**Audience Outcome**
By completing the course, working adults aged 25-45 will be able to:

1. Understand the benefits of using a Roth IRA for tax-free growth.
2. Recognize how to open and fund a Roth IRA account.
3. Make informed decisions about investments within their Roth IRA.

**Module Outline**

| Module # | Title | Description |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | What is a Roth IRA? | Explanation of the basics, benefits, and rules surrounding Roth IRAs. |
| 2 | Limits & Rules for Contributions | Overview of contribution limits, income restrictions, and other essential rules. |
| 3 | How to Open a Roth IRA Account | Step-by-step guide on selecting and opening a Roth IRA account with a reputable provider. |
| 4 | Investing Within Your Roth IRA | Practical advice on choosing the right investments (e.g., stocks, ETFs) for long-term growth. |

**Deliverables List**

1. **Video Course**: A structured series of video lessons (totaling approximately 10 minutes of content), including:
	* Introductory and explanatory videos.
	* Interactive quizzes to reinforce key concepts.
	* Conclusion and next steps.
2. **Transcript PDF**: A downloadable, easy-to-read version of the video course for those who prefer text-based learning.
3. **Bonus Resources** (optional):
	+ Additional worksheets or templates for tracking progress.
	+ Links to recommended financial tools or platforms.

**Pricing Hypothesis**
Given the niche and audience's willingness to invest in their financial knowledge, a reasonable starting price point is $29-$49 per course. This reflects the value of learning about Roth IRAs without overwhelming the audience with an overpriced product.

**Launch Checklist**

1. **Pre-Launch**:
	* Finalize content creation.
	* Set up sales pages (e.g., ClickFunnels, Gumroad).
	* Establish affiliate partnerships.
2. **Launch Day**:
	* Announce launch on social media and relevant online communities.
	* Offer a limited-time discount or promotion to encourage early adoption.
3. **Post-Launch**:
	* Monitor analytics for course engagement and feedback.
	* Improve content based on user input (if necessary).
	* Expand the product line by offering additional financial education courses.

This structured approach allows for efficient production, realistic pricing, and rapid iteration based on customer feedback.
