**Product Concept Pack**

**Product Name:** 90-Day Expense Tracker Challenge

**Product Concept Statement:**
The 90-Day Expense Tracker Challenge is a digital product that guides working adults in identifying and managing their expenses, leading to financial clarity and reduced stress. This challenge consists of a structured program with daily tasks, monthly check-ins, and valuable lessons learned from the creator's own experience tracking every penny for 90 days.

**Audience Outcome:**
By completing the 90-Day Expense Tracker Challenge, working adults will be able to:

* Identify areas where they can cut back on unnecessary expenses
* Develop a sustainable system for tracking their spending
* Improve their financial literacy and confidence in managing their finances

**Module Outline:**

1. **Setup (Days 1-7)**
	* Introduction to the challenge and its benefits
	* Setting up a budgeting system
	* Understanding expense categories
2. **Month 1 (Days 8-30)**
	* Tracking daily expenses
	* Identifying unnecessary expenses
	* Strategies for reducing waste
3. **Month 2 (Days 31-60)**
	* Analyzing spending patterns and identifying areas for improvement
	* Implementing budget-friendly lifestyle changes
	* Introduction to savings strategies
4. **Month 3 (Days 61-90)**
	* Fine-tuning the budgeting system
	* Managing financial stress and emotions
	* Celebrating progress and setting future goals
5. **Lessons (Throughout the Challenge)**
	* Valuable insights and lessons learned from the creator's experience
	* Tips for maintaining momentum and staying on track

**Deliverables:**

1. Email course with daily tasks and monthly check-ins
2. Private Facebook group or community for support and accountability
3. Downloadable worksheets and templates for tracking expenses and creating a budget
4. Access to the creator's private podcast or video series discussing personal finance and expense tracking

**Pricing Hypothesis:**
The 90-Day Expense Tracker Challenge will be priced at $297, with an optional upsell of a one-on-one coaching session for $197.

**Launch Checklist:**

1. Prepare the email course and deliverables
2. Set up the private Facebook group or community
3. Create social media accounts and content calendar
4. Plan and schedule email marketing campaigns
5. Launch the product on a chosen platform (e.g., Gumroad, Teachable)
6. Execute paid advertising (Facebook ads, Google Ads) to target working adults 25-45
7. Monitor progress, gather feedback, and make adjustments as needed
