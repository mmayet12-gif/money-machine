**Product Concept Pack**

**How to Build Wealth on a $40K Salary**

**Product Concept Statement**
Help working adults with a modest income ($40K-$60K) build wealth and improve their financial stability through a structured, step-by-step approach.

**Audience Outcome**
Working adults will develop a clear understanding of how to create a strong financial foundation, achieve significant income growth, and build wealth over time, despite having a relatively low income.

**Module Outline**

1. **Understanding Your Financial Sequence**: Assessing current finances, creating a budget, and identifying areas for improvement.
2. **Prioritizing Debt Repayment**: Strategies for paying off high-interest debt quickly and efficiently.
3. **Building an Emergency Fund**: Creating a safety net to prevent financial shocks.
4. **Maximizing Income**: Increasing earnings through side hustles, salary negotiations, and career development.
5. **Investing for Wealth**: Introduction to investing in stocks, real estate, or other assets.
6. **Automating Your Finances**: Setting up automated systems for savings, investments, and bill payments.

**Deliverables**

* 8 video modules (approx. 30 minutes each)
* Downloadable worksheets and templates
* Private Facebook community for support and Q&A

**Pricing Hypothesis**
Offer the course at an introductory price of $297 for a limited time (2-3 weeks). Later, increase the price to $497-$597.

**Launch Checklist**

1. **Pre-Launch**:
	* Create email list through free webinar or Facebook group
	* Build anticipation with promotional content (social media, blog posts)
2. **Launch Day**:
	* Send launch email to subscribers
	* Offer exclusive bonuses for early adopters
3. **Post-Launch**:
	* Deliver course materials and support within 24-48 hours
	* Encourage student engagement through Q&A sessions and live webinars

This product concept pack provides a clear structure for creating a digital product that addresses the needs of working adults with modest incomes. By focusing on a step-by-step approach, automation, and income growth, we can help our audience build wealth over time, despite their current financial constraints.
