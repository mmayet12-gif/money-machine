**Product Concept Pack: Side Hustles That Actually Pay in 2026**

**Product Concept Statement:**
"Side Hustles That Actually Pay in 2026" is a digital product that provides working adults with a validated list of profitable side hustles, setup checklist, and a first-30-days plan to get started. It aims to help users avoid outdated information and achieve financial success.

**Audience Outcome:**
By the end of the product, users will be able to:

* Identify high-paying side hustles relevant to their skills and interests
* Create a solid foundation for their side hustle with a setup checklist
* Develop a clear plan for the first 30 days to ensure steady progress

**Module Outline:**

1. **Validation Filter**: A quiz to help users identify their strengths, weaknesses, and interests, providing personalized recommendations.
2. **Top Options**: A list of high-paying side hustles validated through market research and user feedback, categorized by skill level and interest.
3. **Setup Checklist**: A step-by-step guide for setting up each recommended side hustle, including necessary tools, resources, and tips.
4. **First 30 Days**: A plan for the initial 30 days of the side hustle journey, including daily tasks, milestones, and motivational content.

**Deliverables List:**

* In-depth report on validation filter results
* Top options list with descriptions and estimated earning potential
* Setup checklist PDF
* First 30 Days plan with daily tasks and milestones

**Pricing Hypothesis:**
Based on the product's value proposition and competition, a price range of $97-$197 is hypothesized. The exact price will be determined through customer feedback and market research.

**Launch Checklist:**

1. **Pre-Launch**
	* Finalize content and deliverables
	* Set up sales funnel and payment gateway
	* Develop email marketing campaign for pre-launch and post-launch promotion
2. **Launch**
	* Launch product on designated date and time
	* Promote on social media, email, and relevant online communities
3. **Post-Launch**
	* Gather customer feedback and reviews
	* Analyze sales and user behavior to refine the product and marketing strategy

Note: The launch checklist focuses on a solo creator's capabilities, prioritizing essential tasks for a successful launch while keeping it manageable.
