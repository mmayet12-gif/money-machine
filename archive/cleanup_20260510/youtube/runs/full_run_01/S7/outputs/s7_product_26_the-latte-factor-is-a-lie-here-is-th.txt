**Product Concept Pack**

**1. Product Concept Statement**

Title: Wealth Shift

Wealth Shift is an online course that challenges the common myth of the "latte factor" and offers actionable advice on how working adults can overcome real wealth bottlenecks to achieve financial freedom.

**2. Audience Outcome**

By the end of the course, learners will be able to:

* Identify and challenge their own misconceptions about personal finance
* Recognize the most significant obstacles to their financial growth
* Develop a tailored plan to tackle these challenges and accelerate their wealth-building journey

**3. Module Outline**

**Module 1: The Latte Factor Myth (30 minutes)**

* Introduction to the "latte factor" myth
* Analysis of its limitations in achieving significant wealth
* Preparing learners for the real bottlenecks ahead

**Module 2: Real Bottlenecks (45 minutes)**

* Overview of common financial obstacles faced by working adults (e.g., high-interest debt, low income growth)
* In-depth examination of the consequences and impact on wealth-building

**Module 3: Better Actions (60 minutes)**

* Practical strategies for addressing real bottlenecks (e.g., debt consolidation, investment education)
* Hands-on exercises to help learners develop a personalized plan

**Module 4: Reframe Your Thinking (45 minutes)**

* Mindset shift from tiny cuts to significant wealth-building actions
* Final thoughts and encouragement for long-term success

**4. Deliverables List**

* 4 video modules (total duration: approximately 2.5 hours)
* Downloadable workbook with exercises and templates
* Private online community for support and discussion
* Email series with bonus content and resources

**5. Pricing Hypothesis**

$297-$497 per participant, given the high-quality content and personalized approach. Initial pricing will be $297 to attract early adopters and gather feedback.

**6. Launch Checklist**

1. **Pre-Launch**:
	* Develop and refine course content (4-6 weeks)
	* Set up online community and email sequence
2. **Launch Preparation**:
	* Plan launch promotions (e.g., webinars, social media contests)
	* Coordinate with potential affiliates or influencers
3. **Launch Day**:
	* Publish the course on a dedicated landing page
	* Share launch announcements across social media channels
4. **Post-Launch**:
	* Gather feedback from early participants and make improvements
	* Develop additional resources (e.g., blog posts, podcasts) to support learners
