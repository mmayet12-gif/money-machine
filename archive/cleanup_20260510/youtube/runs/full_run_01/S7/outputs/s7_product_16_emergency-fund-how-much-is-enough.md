**Product Concept Pack: Emergency Fund: How Much Is Enough?**

**1. Product Concept Statement**

Emergency Fund: How Much Is Enough? is a digital product that helps working adults 25-45 build an effective emergency fund, customized to their individual risk factors and financial goals. This comprehensive guide empowers users with a clear understanding of what constitutes "enough" for their unique situation.

**2. Audience Outcome**

Users will be able to:

* Assess their personal risk factors (e.g., job security, debt, health)
* Determine an optimal emergency fund target size based on their risk profile
* Choose suitable storage options for their emergency fund
* Create a tailored build schedule to achieve their goal

**3. Module Outline**

Module 1: Understanding Risk Factors (15-20 minutes)

* Identifying and quantifying personal risk factors
* How risk impacts emergency fund requirements

Module 2: Target Size Calculation (10-15 minutes)

* Introduction to the "50/30/20" rule
* Adjusting for individual risk factors
* Calculating a realistic target size

Module 3: Storage Options Exploration (10-15 minutes)

* Overview of popular storage options (high-yield savings, CDs, etc.)
* Choosing the best option based on user's needs and preferences

Module 4: Building an Emergency Fund (20-25 minutes)

* Creating a tailored build schedule
* Strategies for accelerating progress
* Overcoming common obstacles

**4. Deliverables List**

Deliverables will include:

* A comprehensive guide document (PDF) detailing the emergency fund strategy
* A spreadsheet template for calculating target size and building schedule
* Bonus content: "Emergency Fund Tracker" printable worksheet and a list of recommended resources

**5. Pricing Hypothesis**

Pricing hypothesis: Offer the digital product at $29, targeting a moderate price point that balances value with affordability.

**6. Launch Checklist**

Pre-Launch:

1. Complete high-quality content creation
2. Develop and test the spreadsheet template
3. Set up affiliate partnerships (if applicable)
4. Prepare email marketing campaigns for launch promotion

Launch:

1. Publish the digital product on platforms like Gumroad or Sellfy
2. Share launch details on social media, email newsletters, and relevant forums
3. Offer a limited-time discount to early adopters
4. Monitor analytics and gather feedback from users

Post-Launch:

1. Refine content based on user feedback
2. Expand affiliate partnerships or consider sponsored content
3. Plan for future product updates or expansion (e.g., additional modules)
