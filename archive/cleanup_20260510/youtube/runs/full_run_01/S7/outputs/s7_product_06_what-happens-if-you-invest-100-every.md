**Product Concept Pack**

**Product Concept Statement**
What Happens If You Invest $100 Every Month is a digital product that helps working adults 25-45 understand the power of small, consistent investing and take their first steps towards building wealth. Through a combination of interactive tools, real-life scenarios, and practical advice, this product empowers users to make informed decisions about their finances and start investing with confidence.

**Audience Outcome**
By the end of the product, working adults will be able to:

* Understand how small, consistent investing can lead to significant long-term wealth
* Identify the most suitable investment platforms for their needs
* Create a personalized plan for starting their investment journey

**Module Outline**

1. **Introduction**
	* The power of compounding math explained in simple terms
	* Why small monthly investments can add up over time
2. **Compounding Math Explained**
	* Interactive calculator to visualize the impact of small investments
	* Examples and scenarios illustrating the effects of compounding interest
3. **Investment Scenarios**
	* Real-life examples of how different investment amounts and timeframes affect outcomes
	* Interactive quizzes to help users identify their own financial goals and risk tolerance
4. **Platform Choices**
	* Overview of popular investment platforms (e.g., robo-advisors, brokerages)
	* Comparison of fees, features, and user experience
5. **First Investment: Getting Started**
	* Step-by-step guide to opening an investment account and making the first investment
	* Tips for overcoming common obstacles and staying motivated

**Deliverables List**

1. 10-15 minute video series covering each module
2. Interactive calculator (Google Sheets or similar) to calculate compounding math
3. Downloadable workbook with exercises, scenarios, and prompts for users to apply the concepts to their own finances
4. Private Facebook group or community for Q&A, support, and motivation

**Pricing Hypothesis**
Based on a survey of similar products in the personal finance niche, we hypothesize that What Happens If You Invest $100 Every Month will appeal to working adults 25-45 who are interested in investing but lack confidence. We propose an initial price point of $297 (one-time payment) or $99/month (subscription-based), with a discount offered for early adopters and loyal customers.

**Launch Checklist**

1. Pre-launch promotion:
	* Social media campaign to create buzz
	* Email list building and nurturing
	* Content marketing to attract potential customers
2. Launch day:
	* Live webinar or Q&A session to address questions and concerns
	* Limited-time discount for early adopters
	* Review and ratings system in place to collect feedback
3. Post-launch:
	* Regular updates, additional content, and community engagement
	* Email follow-up sequence to nurture leads and encourage investment
	* Continued social media promotion and outreach
