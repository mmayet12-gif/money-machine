**Product Concept Pack**

**Product Concept Statement**
"Credit Score 800+ Secrets Nobody Tells You" is an online course and resource guide that empowers working adults to achieve an exceptional credit score by providing actionable strategies, a step-by-step plan, and essential tools.

**Audience Outcome**
Working adults (25-45) will be able to:

* Understand the key drivers of their credit score
* Identify and fix order their financial mistakes
* Create a personalized timeline for achieving an 800+ credit score
* Use effective tools to monitor and maintain their progress

**Module Outline**

1. **Credit Score Fundamentals**
	* Understanding credit scores (FICO, VantageScore)
	* How credit scoring works
2. **The Top 5 Credit Score Drivers**
	* Payment history (35%)
	* Credit utilization (30%)
	* Length of credit history (15%)
	* New credit inquiries (10%)
	* Credit mix (10%)
3. **Fix Order Your Financial Mistakes**
	* Identifying and addressing errors on your report
	* Disputing and resolving disputes
4. **Creating a Personalized Plan for Success**
	* Setting realistic goals and timelines
	* Prioritizing efforts and tracking progress
5. **Essential Tools for Credit Score Management**
	* Monitoring credit reports and scores
	* Utilizing budgeting and financial apps

**Deliverables List**

1. 8-module online course (video lessons, PDF guides)
2. Private Facebook group for community support
3. Downloadable worksheet: "Credit Score Action Plan"
4. Exclusive interview with a credit expert
5. Bonus resource: "Top 10 Credit Score Myths Debunked"

**Pricing Hypothesis**
We will offer two pricing tiers to cater to different segments of our audience:

* Basic: $297 (self-study online course)
* Premium: $497 (includes private coaching, personalized plan review)

**Launch Checklist**

1. **Content Creation**
	* Develop high-quality video lessons and PDF guides
	* Create engaging social media content for promotion
2. **Marketing Strategy**
	* Establish a strong online presence (Facebook, Instagram, Twitter)
	* Collaborate with influencers in the personal finance space
3. **Launch Preparation**
	* Set up course hosting platform (e.g., Teachable, Thinkific)
	* Configure payment gateway and email marketing integrations

**Timeline**

* Month 1-2: Content creation and marketing strategy development
* Month 3-4: Launch preparation and setup
* Month 5: Launch and promote the course
