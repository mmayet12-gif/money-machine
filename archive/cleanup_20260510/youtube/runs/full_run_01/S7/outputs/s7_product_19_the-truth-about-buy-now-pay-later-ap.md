**Product Concept Pack**

**Product Name:** Buy Now, Pay Later: The Hidden Truth

**Product Concept Statement:**
"Buy Now Pay Later: The Hidden Truth" is an online educational resource that exposes the dangers of using buy now pay later (BNPL) apps, empowering working adults to make informed financial decisions and break free from debt.

**Audience Outcome:**
Users will understand the true costs and risks associated with BNPL apps, develop strategies to avoid unnecessary debt, and feel motivated to adopt healthier financial habits.

**Module Outline:**

1. **Introduction to BNPL Apps**
	* How BNPL apps work
	* Examples of popular BNPL apps
2. **The Behavior Effects of BNPL**
	* The psychological triggers that drive over-spending
	* How BNPL apps manipulate user behavior
3. **Hidden Costs and Fees**
	* Interest rates and late payment fees
	* Other charges to watch out for
4. **Alternatives to BNPL Apps**
	* Budgeting techniques for responsible spending
	* Alternative financial tools for managing expenses

**Deliverables List:**

1. A 30-minute video series, broken into the four modules outlined above
2. A downloadable PDF guide summarizing key takeaways and next steps
3. A companion worksheet to help users track their spending habits
4. A private Facebook group or online community for support and discussion

**Pricing Hypothesis:**
The product will be priced at $29.99, with a 14-day money-back guarantee. The goal is to monetize the audience through ads on the video series and companion PDF guide.

**Launch Checklist:**

1. Develop and film the video content (4-6 weeks)
2. Create and design the companion PDF guide and worksheet (2-3 weeks)
3. Set up the private Facebook group or online community (1 week)
4. Launch the product on a dedicated website, including sales page and checkout process (1-2 weeks)
5. Promote the product through social media, email marketing, and paid advertising (Ongoing)

**Timeline:**

* Development phase: 8-12 weeks
* Testing and refinement phase: 2-4 weeks
* Launch preparation phase: 2-4 weeks

Note: This is a solo creator-focused project, so we'll prioritize speed to first sale and keep the scope manageable.
