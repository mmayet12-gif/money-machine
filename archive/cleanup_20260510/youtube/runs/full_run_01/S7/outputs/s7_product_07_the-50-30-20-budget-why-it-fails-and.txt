**Product Concept Pack**

**Product Name:** Budget Fix

**Product Concept Statement:**
Budget Fix is a practical and motivational digital product that helps working adults (25-45) overcome the limitations of the 50/30/20 budget rule by providing a personalized approach to creating a balanced budget.

**Audience Outcome:**
Users will be able to:

1. Understand why the traditional 50/30/20 budget formula often fails
2. Create a customized budget that aligns with their individual financial goals and expenses
3. Develop a monthly review process to ensure they stay on track

**Module Outline:**

Module 1: The 50/30/20 Rule - A Brief Recap (5-7 minutes)

* Introduce the 50/30/20 rule and its limitations
* Discuss common failure points

Module 2: Why Your Expenses Don't Match the Formula (15-20 minutes)

* Explore real-life scenarios where the formula falls short
* Identify key factors to consider when creating a personalized budget

Module 3: Creating Your Customized Budget (30-40 minutes)

* Walk users through a step-by-step process for creating a balanced budget based on their individual needs
* Include templates and worksheets to support the creation of a customized budget plan

Module 4: Monthly Review and Adjustment (15-20 minutes)

* Teach users how to regularly review their budget and make adjustments as needed
* Provide tips for staying motivated and accountable throughout the process

**Deliverables List:**

1. Video modules with clear instructions and examples
2. Downloadable templates and worksheets for creating a customized budget plan
3. Private Facebook group or community support for users to connect, ask questions, and share progress
4. Monthly email check-ins with additional resources and guidance

**Pricing Hypothesis:**
Based on the medium volume of the niche and the digital product format, we propose an initial price point of $49-$79. This will allow us to test the market and make adjustments as needed.

**Launch Checklist:**

1. Finalize video content and formatting
2. Develop and design templates and worksheets
3. Set up private Facebook group or community support
4. Create email sequence for monthly check-ins
5. Prepare landing page and sales funnel with clear messaging and instructions
6. Establish payment processing and delivery system
7. Launch product on designated date, promoting through social media, email marketing, and relevant online communities

This launch plan prioritizes speed to first sale while maintaining a realistic scope for a solo creator. The product concept pack includes essential components for delivering value to the target audience, with room for future expansion and improvement based on user feedback and performance metrics.
