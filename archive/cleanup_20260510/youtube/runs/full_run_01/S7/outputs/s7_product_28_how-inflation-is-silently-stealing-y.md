**Product Concept Pack**

**Product Name:** ShieldSavings

**Product Concept Statement:**
ShieldSavings is an online course that empowers working adults to safeguard their savings from inflation, providing actionable insights and practical strategies for preserving purchasing power.

**Audience Outcome:**
By the end of the course, learners will:

* Understand the impact of inflation on their savings
* Recognize real-world examples of inflation's erosive effect
* Learn effective ways to protect and grow their savings
* Develop a tailored plan to shield their financial goals from inflation

**Module Outline:**

1. **Introduction**
	* Defining inflation and its effects
	* Why saving is not enough in an inflationary environment
2. **The Silent Thief: How Inflation Affects Your Savings**
	* Real-life examples of inflation's impact on savings
	* Common myths and misconceptions about inflation
3. **Assessing the Damage: Understanding Inflation's True Cost**
	* Calculating real loss due to inflation
	* The importance of understanding opportunity costs
4. **Protecting Your Purchasing Power**
	* Introduction to interest rates and inflation relationships
	* Strategies for preserving savings, including inflation-indexed accounts and alternative investments
5. **Portfolio Angle: Diversification and Inflation Protection**
	* How to incorporate inflation-hedging assets into your portfolio
	* Balancing risk and return in an inflationary environment

**Deliverables List:**

1. 6 video lessons (approx. 45 minutes each)
2. Downloadable worksheets for calculating real loss due to inflation and creating a protection plan
3. Private Facebook community for discussion, Q&A, and support
4. Bonus content: "Inflation-Proofing Your Emergency Fund" guide

**Pricing Hypothesis:**
The course will be priced at $297, with an early-bird discount of 20% (limited to the first 50 enrollments). Affiliate commissions will be set at 15%.

**Launch Checklist:**

1. Finalize content creation and editing
2. Set up course hosting platform (e.g., Teachable)
3. Create email marketing campaigns for promotion and lead generation
4. Establish affiliate partnerships with relevant financial services providers
5. Launch course on a Tuesday or Wednesday to maximize visibility and engagement

**Additional Considerations:**

* To ensure speed to market, the first version of ShieldSavings will focus on the core content outlined above.
* Future updates and expansions can include more advanced topics, live sessions, or premium coaching services.
* Continuous customer feedback and support will be essential for building a loyal community around the course.
