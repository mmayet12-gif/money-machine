**Product Concept Pack**

**Product Concept Statement**
"Debt Avalanche vs Snowball: The Truth" is a digital product that helps working adults 25-45 make informed decisions about their debt repayment strategy, combining mathematical calculations with behavioral insights to provide a clear and practical guide for achieving financial freedom.

**Audience Outcome**
Users will understand the key differences between the debt avalanche and snowball methods, know how to apply math and behavior to inform their decision, and be empowered to take control of their debt repayment journey.

**Module Outline**

I. **Introduction** (5-7 minutes)

* Brief overview of debt repayment strategies
* Importance of making an informed decision

II. **Math Explained** (10-12 minutes)

* Explanation of the debt avalanche method
* Explanation of the snowball method
* Comparison of both methods using real-life examples

III. **Behavioral Factors** (8-10 minutes)

* Discussion on why humans often choose the snowball method despite its flaws
* Strategies for overcoming emotional barriers to debt repayment

IV. **Decision Guide** (5-7 minutes)

* Step-by-step guide for choosing the best debt repayment strategy
* Tips and resources for implementing the chosen strategy

**Deliverables List**

1. A 30-minute video explaining the debt avalanche vs snowball methods, including math examples and behavioral insights.
2. A downloadable PDF or spreadsheet providing step-by-step instructions for creating a personalized debt repayment plan.
3. Access to a private Facebook group or community for support and accountability.

**Pricing Hypothesis**
The product will be priced at $29, with an upsell offer of $49 for users who want additional support and guidance through the private community.

**Launch Checklist**

1. **Pre-Launch (2 weeks)**

* Create a landing page with opt-in form
* Develop email sequence to nurture leads
* Launch social media campaign on Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, and LinkedIn

2. **Launch Day (Week 3-4)**

* Send out launch email to subscribers
* Share content on relevant online communities
* Host a live webinar or Q&A session

3. **Post-Launch (Weeks 5-8)**

* Analyze sales data and adjust marketing strategy as needed
* Create additional content for the private community, such as workbooks or templates
* Plan for future product updates or expansions
