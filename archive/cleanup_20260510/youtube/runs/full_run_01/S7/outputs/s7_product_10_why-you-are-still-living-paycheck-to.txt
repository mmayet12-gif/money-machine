Here's the product concept pack:

**Product Concept Statement**

"Break free from living paycheck to paycheck with our step-by-step system, designed to help you understand and overcome the root causes of your financial stress. By identifying and addressing the real issues holding you back, you'll gain control over your finances and achieve long-term peace of mind."

**Audience Outcome**

* Understand the root causes of their financial struggles
* Create a personalized cashflow map to visualize their financial situation
* Develop a clear plan to fix common financial problems
* Establish habit-forming routines to maintain progress
* Gain confidence in managing their finances

**Module Outline**

1. **Understanding Paycheck Stress**
	* Defining the problem and its impact on mental health
	* Debunking common myths about living paycheck to paycheck
2. **Root Causes of Financial Struggles**
	* Identifying personal spending habits vs. needs
	* Examining income, expenses, debt, and savings
	* Understanding the role of emotions in financial decision-making
3. **Creating a Cashflow Map**
	* Introducing cashflow mapping as a visual tool for understanding finances
	* Guided exercise: creating a personalized cashflow map
4. **Fix Sequence: Prioritizing Financial Goals**
	* Identifying short-term and long-term goals
	* Creating a prioritized list of financial objectives
5. **Habit Guardrails: Building Lasting Change**
	* Understanding the importance of habits in achieving financial stability
	* Developing strategies for creating and maintaining positive financial habits

**Deliverables**

* 5-module video course (approx. 2 hours total)
* Downloadable cashflow map template
* Access to a private Facebook group or community forum
* Bonus materials: additional resources, templates, and worksheets

**Pricing Hypothesis**

Based on similar products in the personal finance space, we'll start with a price point of $297-$497. This will allow us to reach a wider audience while still providing sufficient value for our customers.

**Launch Checklist**

1. Finalize product content and design
2. Set up sales page and marketing materials
3. Establish payment processing and affiliate program (if applicable)
4. Create social media campaigns and promotional graphics
5. Launch email marketing campaign to subscribers and list
6. Monitor analytics and customer feedback for future improvements

This product concept pack should give you a solid foundation for creating a successful digital product in the personal finance niche. Remember to keep your scope realistic, focus on delivering value to your customers, and prioritize speed to first sale. Good luck with your project!
