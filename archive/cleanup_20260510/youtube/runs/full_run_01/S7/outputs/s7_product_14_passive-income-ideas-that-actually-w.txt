**Product Concept Pack**

**Product Name:** Passive Income Ideas That Actually Work in 2026

**Product Concept Statement:**
"Passive Income Ideas That Actually Work in 2026" is a digital product that provides working adults with actionable, evidence-based strategies for creating sustainable passive income streams. This product serves as a reality check on common myths and offers practical guidance on implementing proven models.

**Audience Outcome:**
By the end of this product, our audience will have gained clarity on what truly works in generating passive income and will be equipped with a clear plan to implement these strategies, resulting in increased financial stability and reduced stress.

**Module Outline:**

1. **Reality Check (10-15% of content)**
	* Debunking common myths about passive income
	* Understanding the importance of realistic expectations
2. **Best Models (40-50% of content)**
	* In-depth exploration of top-performing passive income models for 2026
	* Analysis of successful case studies and expert opinions
3. **Startup Effort (20-25% of content)**
	* Breakdown of initial investment required for each model
	* Guidance on minimizing startup costs without sacrificing results
4. **Execution Plan (15-20% of content)**
	* Step-by-step implementation plan for each passive income model
	* Tools and resources for tracking progress and overcoming obstacles

**Deliverables List:**

1. In-depth video lessons (6-8 modules)
2. Downloadable worksheets and templates
3. Access to a private online community for support and Q&A
4. Bonus content, including:
	* A comprehensive passive income model comparison guide
	* A list of recommended books and resources

**Pricing Hypothesis:**
The product will be priced at $497, with discounts available for early bird customers ($297) and bundle deals for premium features (e.g., 1-on-1 coaching).

**Launch Checklist:**

1. **Pre-Launch (Weeks 1-4)**
	* Content creation and review
	* Email list building and marketing setup
2. **Launch Week (Week 5)**
	* Launch email campaign and social media promotion
	* Countdown timer on sales page
3. **Post-Launch (Weeks 6-8)**
	* Customer support and community engagement
	* Analysis of results and adjustments to marketing strategy

**Assumptions:**

1. High volume of content will be created within a short timeframe.
2. Email list building and marketing efforts will yield a moderate number of subscribers.
3. Pricing will be competitive while allowing for profit margins.

This product concept pack provides a solid foundation for creating a high-quality, in-demand digital product that addresses the specific needs of working adults seeking passive income solutions.
