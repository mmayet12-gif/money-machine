Here is a production-ready product concept pack:

**Product Concept Statement**
"How to Save $1,000 in 30 Days (Any Income)" is a digital program designed to help working adults save money quickly and effectively, regardless of their income level. This program provides a clear, step-by-step plan to achieve financial goals in just 30 days.

**Audience Outcome**
By the end of this program, participants will be able to:

* Set and achieve a specific savings goal within a short timeframe
* Identify and eliminate unnecessary expenses ("leaks") that hinder their progress
* Automate their savings process for sustained results
* Develop a daily habit of tracking their financial wins

**Module Outline**

1. **Goal Setting**: Understand the importance of clear goals, set a realistic target, and create an action plan (10-15 minutes)
2. **Cutting Leaks**: Identify areas to reduce unnecessary expenses, prioritize needs over wants, and implement cost-saving strategies (20-25 minutes)
3. **Automating Savings**: Set up automatic transfers, explore savings apps, and choose the right tools for consistent progress (20-25 minutes)
4. **Tracking Daily Wins**: Develop a daily habit of monitoring finances, celebrating small victories, and staying motivated (15-20 minutes)

**Deliverables List**

1. 30-Day Challenge Workbook: A downloadable PDF guide to accompany participants throughout the program
2. Video Series: Four video lessons (10-15 minutes each) covering the module outline topics
3. Savings Tracker Template: A customizable spreadsheet to help participants track their progress and stay motivated
4. Exclusive Community Access: A private Facebook group or forum for support, motivation, and community sharing

**Pricing Hypothesis**
Given the high demand for personal finance solutions and the limited scope of this program (digital-only), a competitive pricing strategy could be:

* Early Bird Special: $19-$29 (limited-time offer)
* Regular Price: $39-$49
* Bundle or Upsell Opportunities: Offer additional resources, such as a comprehensive savings plan template or one-on-one coaching sessions

**Launch Checklist**

1. **Content Creation**: Finalize video lessons, workbook, and tracker template
2. **Marketing Materials**: Develop social media graphics, email marketing campaign, and sales page content
3. **Pricing Strategy**: Decide on pricing options and create a payment gateway setup
4. **Promotion**: Schedule social media promotions, influencer outreach (if applicable), and email marketing campaigns to target audience
5. **Launch Date**: Set a specific launch date and time for the program's debut
6. **Post-Launch Evaluation**: Monitor sales, gather feedback, and refine future updates or expansions
