Here is a product concept pack for "What a Financial Advisor Will Not Tell You":

**Product Concept Statement**

"What a Financial Advisor Will Not Tell You" is an online course and resource hub that exposes the hidden realities of working with financial advisors, empowering working adults to make informed decisions about their financial futures.

**Audience Outcome**

By completing the course, working adults 25-45 will be able to:

* Identify potential conflicts of interest in advisor-client relationships
* Understand how advisor incentives can impact investment recommendations
* Ask the right questions to ensure alignment with their financial goals
* Develop a DIY mindset to take control of their finances

**Module Outline**

 Module 1: The Advisor's Hidden Agenda (15-minute video)

* Introduction to advisor fees and fee models
* Exploring conflicts of interest in the financial industry
* Setting clear expectations for advisor-client relationships

 Module 2: Decoding Advisor Incentives (20-minute video)

* Understanding how advisors are compensated
* Recognizing the impact of incentives on investment recommendations
* Case studies illustrating potential pitfalls

 Module 3: Navigating Financial Jargon (30-minute interactive quiz)

* Building confidence in understanding financial terms and concepts
* Identifying key questions to ask advisors
* DIY threshold assessment to gauge readiness for self-managed finances

 Module 4: Asking the Right Questions (45-minute video interview with a certified financial planner)

* Expert guidance on crafting effective queries for advisors
* Best practices for evaluating advisor recommendations
* Q&A session addressing common concerns and misconceptions

**Deliverables List**

1. Course homepage and introductory content
2. Four modules as described above, each including:
	* Interactive videos (10-30 minutes)
	* Quizzes or assessments (10-30 minutes)
	* Downloadable resources (e.g., worksheets, eBooks)
3. Private online community for discussion and support
4. Email newsletter with additional tips, resources, and updates

**Pricing Hypothesis**

Initial pricing: $297 (one-time payment)

Rationale:

* The course content is comprehensive but focused on a specific niche (financial advisor secrets).
* The target audience has some disposable income as working adults 25-45.
* Monetization strategies (ads and affiliate marketing) will be implemented to generate recurring revenue.

**Launch Checklist**

Week 1-2: Set up online platform, course content, and private community

Week 3-4: Develop email marketing campaign for pre-launch and launch phases

Week 5: Launch course and email newsletter series

Week 6-8: Optimize and refine course content based on user feedback

Week 9-12: Implement affiliate marketing and ad strategies
