**Product Concept Pack**

**1. Product Concept Statement**

**HSA Pro**: Unlock the Triple Tax Advantage of Your HSA Account with a Simple, Step-by-Step Guide to Maximizing Earnings.

As a working adult navigating the complexities of personal finance, maximizing the potential of your Health Savings Account (HSA) can significantly impact long-term financial stability. **HSA Pro** is an online course designed to educate and empower individuals in making informed decisions about their HSA investments, leveraging the account's triple tax advantages for a more secure financial future.

**2. Audience Outcome**

By completing **HSA Pro**, working adults 25-45 will be able to:

- Understand the eligibility criteria and benefits of an HSA
- Develop a clear investment strategy tailored to their financial goals
- Learn how to navigate common challenges in maximizing HSA earnings
- Achieve peace of mind knowing they're making the most of their triple tax-advantaged account

**3. Module Outline**

**Module 1: Understanding Your HSA**
- Eligibility and IRS guidelines
- Benefits of an HSA, including triple tax advantage

**Module 2: Investing Strategically**
- Overview of investment options (stocks, ETFs, mutual funds)
- Risk management strategies for HSA investments
- Tax implications on withdrawals and earnings

**Module 3: Navigating Limits and Challenges**
- Understanding contribution limits and potential penalties
- Strategies for managing liquidity while investing in an HSA

**Module 4: Putting Your Knowledge into Action**
- Creating a personal investment plan tailored to your goals
- Setting up automatic investments within your HSA account

**Deliverables**

- **HSA Pro** online course (video lessons, PDF guides)
- Personalized worksheet for creating a customized investment plan
- Access to a private community forum for support and Q&A

**5. Pricing Hypothesis**

Pricing will be tiered based on the level of personalization and support offered:

- Basic Package: $297 ($197 introductory offer) - access to online course and PDF guides.
- Premium Package: $497 ($397 introductory offer) - includes all basic package benefits plus personalized investment plan creation and community forum access.
