**Product Concept Pack**

**The Pay Yourself First Strategy (Step-by-Step)**

**Product Concept Statement**
Help working adults 25-45 master the "Pay Yourself First" method, a simple yet effective strategy to improve their financial health and reach their savings goals.

**Audience Outcome**
By following this step-by-step guide, working adults will be able to:

1. Set up an automatic transfer system that prioritizes their finances
2. Create a customized plan to save for short-term and long-term goals
3. Monitor and adjust their progress to achieve financial freedom

**Module Outline**

I. Introduction to the Pay Yourself First Method (10 minutes)

* Explanation of the benefits and simplicity of the method
* Setting clear savings goals

II. Account Setup (30 minutes)

* Choosing the right bank accounts for automatic transfers
* Linking accounts for seamless transactions

III. Transfer Rules (20 minutes)

* Determining transfer amounts based on income and expenses
* Establishing a consistent schedule

IV. Weekly Check-in (10 minutes)

* Reviewing progress towards goals
* Adjusting transfer rules as needed

**Deliverables List**

1. A 4-module video course ( approximately 60-90 minutes of content)
2. Downloadable worksheets for account setup and transfer rule creation
3. Private Facebook group or community support for accountability and Q&A

**Pricing Hypothesis**
$297 - $497 per person, with a possible tiered pricing structure for bundle deals or group coaching.

**Launch Checklist**

1. **Pre-launch**:
	* Create a private Facebook group for early supporters
	* Share exclusive content to build anticipation
	* Offer a limited-time discount for the first 50 sign-ups
2. **Launch Day**:
	* Email campaign announcing the launch and offering exclusive bonuses
	* Social media promotion across platforms
	* Live Q&A session on social media or private Facebook group
3. **Post-launch**:
	* Regular content updates in the private Facebook group
	* Webinars or live sessions to review progress and answer questions
	* Affiliate partnerships for complementary products or services

This product concept pack is designed to be a scalable, high-ticket digital product that can be sold through affiliate partnerships, with potential for future upsells or bundle deals. The low volume niche allows for targeted marketing efforts, while the step-by-step format ensures a clear and actionable learning experience for customers.
