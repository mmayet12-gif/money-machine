# Daily Operations Checklist

Use this once per day while executing Wave 1.

## Morning (30 minutes)

- Check yesterday metrics from YouTube Studio and email platform.
- Mark top-performing topic and move it up in queue.
- Confirm today publish asset files exist in `S1` to `S7` output folders.
- Finalize title and thumbnail before upload.

## Publishing Block (90 minutes)

- Publish long video (if scheduled today).
- Publish one Shorts asset and one Reels asset from same topic.
- Publish blog companion post and embed long video.
- Add affiliate CTA block to video description, blog, and email.

## Distribution Block (30 minutes)

- Post to primary channels and social copy variants.
- Add pinned comment and first reply on YouTube.
- Send topic email from sequence.

## End of Day (20 minutes)

- Update queue status in `PUBLISH_QUEUE_WAVE1.csv`.
- Record winner/loser notes (hook, title, thumbnail, CTA).
- Set tomorrow topic and preload files.

## Non-Negotiables

- One primary CTA per piece of content.
- Same topic across all channels for each publish day.
- Do not skip analytics review.
- Keep the weekly schedule simple and consistent.
