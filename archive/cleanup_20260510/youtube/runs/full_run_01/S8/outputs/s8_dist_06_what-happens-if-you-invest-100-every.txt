**14-Day Repurposing and Distribution Pack for "What Happens If You Invest $100 Every Month"**

**Day 1-2: Content Creation**

1. Publish a blog post on your website:
	* Title: What Happens If You Invest $100 Every Month
	* Content: Explain the power of compounding math, provide scenarios, and discuss platform choices.
	* Meta Description: Discover how investing just $100 monthly can add up to a fortune over time.
2. Create a Facebook post snippet for publishing on Facebook:
	* "Did you know that small, consistent investments can lead to massive returns? Learn more about the power of compounding math and start building your wealth today! [link to blog post]"
3. Prepare an Instagram caption snippet for publishing on Instagram:
	* "Investing just $100 a month can add up to a fortune over time! Compounding math is real, but it's not just about the numbers - it's about creating financial freedom. Learn more and start your journey today! [link to blog post] #invest100"

**Day 3-4: Cross-Promotion**

1. Share the Facebook post on Twitter:
	* "Investing $100 a month can lead to massive returns over time! Learn more about compounding math and get started today! [link to blog post]"
2. Publish the Instagram caption as an Instagram post.
3. Create a LinkedIn article snippet for publishing on LinkedIn:
	* "Are you looking for ways to grow your wealth? Investing just $100 a month can add up to a fortune over time, thanks to compounding math. Learn more and start building your financial future today! [link to blog post]"

**Day 5-6: Repurposing**

1. Convert the blog post into an infographic:
	* Highlight key points about compounding math and investing $100 a month.
2. Create a Twitter thread discussing the benefits of small, consistent investments:
	* Tweet 1: Did you know that investing just $100 a month can lead to massive returns over time?
	* Tweet 2: Compounding math is real! Learn more about how it works and start building your wealth today!
	* Tweet 3: Don't let money worries hold you back - invest in yourself and your future!

**Day 7-8: Engagement**

1. Host a Facebook Live or Instagram Live session discussing investing $100 a month:
	* Answer viewer questions, provide expert advice, and promote the blog post.
2. Publish a YouTube video explaining compounding math and its benefits:
	* Share personal anecdotes, examples, and visual aids to make the content engaging.

**Day 9-10: Republishing**

1. Re-publish the blog post on Medium:
	* Customize the title, meta description, and content for Medium's audience.
2. Publish a LinkedIn Pulse article summarizing key points from the original blog post:
	* Tailor the language and format to suit LinkedIn's professional audience.

**Day 11-12: Cross-Promotion**

1. Share user-generated content (UGC) on Facebook and Instagram:
	* Encourage readers to share their own stories of investing $100 a month.
2. Publish a guest post on a prominent personal finance blog:
	* Share the expertise and knowledge built in the original blog post.

**Day 13-14: Retargeting**

1. Create an email newsletter with a summary of the content:
	* Invite subscribers to share their thoughts, ask questions, or engage further.
2. Publish a final Facebook post summarizing key takeaways from the campaign:
	* Encourage readers to continue investing $100 a month and building their wealth.
